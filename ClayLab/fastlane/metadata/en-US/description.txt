WHY POTTERS USE IT

Your studio brain is already full. ClayLab is the second brain that remembers what clay body shrank how much, which glaze you dipped twice instead of once, what cone the kiln was running when it came out that blue.

WHAT YOU TRACK

• Seven-stage lifecycle: Idea → Formed → Greenware → Bisque → Glazed → Finished (plus Archived). One tap on the stage chip advances a piece.

• Glaze layers: unlimited per piece. Capture color, inside or outside, dipped / brushed / poured / sprayed / sponged / wax-resist, number of coats, and notes.

• Firing log: bisque or glaze (oxidation, reduction, raku, pit, salt/soda), cone, date, kiln, ramp/hold notes.

• Dimensions & weight: metric or imperial, toggle any time.

• Photos: snap the piece at each stage. Compare trim profiles and glaze results over time.

• Shrinkage calculator: measure wet and after firing — ClayLab does the math.

• Notes: trim depth, stamp location, what surprised you, what to try next time.

• History timeline: every stage change, glaze addition, firing log, and note in one scrollable diary per piece.

DESIGN FOR THE STUDIO

Large tap targets for slippery fingers. A warm, earthy palette that doesn't blow out your eyes under studio lights. Private, local-first — everything lives on your device.
