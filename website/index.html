G

Glaze layer engine

Capture color, inside or outside, dipped / brushed / poured / sprayed / sponged / wax-resist, coat count, and notes. Unlimited layers per piece.

🔥

Firing log

Bisque or glaze (oxidation, reduction, raku, pit, salt/soda), cone, date, kiln, ramp and hold notes. Everything you wish you'd written down last time.

📷

Photos per piece

Snap the piece at each stage. Compare trim profiles, glaze results, and shrinkage over time. Photos live on your device only.

%

Shrinkage calculator

Measure wet, measure after firing. Stoneware does the math so you stop doing it on napkins.

⚖️

Metric or imperial

Grams or ounces, centimeters or inches. Toggle any time. Your data is stored once, displayed however you think.

📓

History timeline

Every stage change, glaze addition, firing log, and note in one scrollable diary per piece. Your memory, externalized.
